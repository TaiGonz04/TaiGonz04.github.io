A Cookie Run: Kingdom Guide!

A Cookie Guide!

The mobile game that I am currently obsessing over is Cookie Run: Kingdom! The object of the game is to build and expand your own kindgom, while also creating your own team from a variety of cookies to choose from. Ever since the game made its debut in 2021, the developers of the game have only been coming out with stronger and indestructable cookies. This has made advancing levels and in game arena battles, more difficult to complete. As a result, people who grind at the game, have turned to using only cookie in the meta. What is the meta you may ask? The meta refers to a set of strategies that are used on characters and builds that are most effective to advancing in a game.
