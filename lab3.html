A Cookie Run: Kingdom Guide!

A Cookie Guide!

The mobile game that I am currently obsessing over is Cookie Run: Kingdom! The object of the game is to build and expand your own kindgom, while also creating your own team from a variety of cookies to choose from. Ever since the game made its debut in 2021, the developers of the game have only been coming out with stronger and indestructable cookies. This has made advancing levels and in game arena battles, more difficult to complete. As a result, people who grind at the game, have turned to using only cookie in the meta. What is the meta you may ask? The meta refers to a set of strategies that are used on characters and builds that are most effective to advancing in a game. Below will be a grid showing the top six cookies, that are in the games meta currently!



The Cookie Meta!

Burning Spice Cookie!

  • Max out to Level 90!
  • 5x Searing Raspberrys for toppings
  • Legendary Charge Beascuit
  • Burning Spice Cookie takes first place, on top of the cookie run meta, as an S+ tier cookie. As a Charge offense cookie, he needs Searing Raspberrys to increase his attack stats. At level 90, his cookie will be maxed out to his highest potential power. While his Beascuit will increase multiple stats at once, from anywhere from a 5% increase to a 10% increase!

Pure Vanilla Cookie: Awakened!

  • Max out to Level 90!
  • 5x Solid Almond for toppings
  • Legendary Tainted Gleaming Sweet Beascuit
  • Now Awakened, Pure Vanilla Cookie takes second place tier in the meta! Pure Vanilla is classified as a healer cookie, he needs Searing Almonds to increase his damage resistant stats, while in battle. At level 90, his cookie will be maxed out to his highest potential power. While his Beascuit will increase multiple stats at once, from anywhere from a 4% increase to a 20% increase in stats!
Shadow Milk Cookie
  • Max out to Level 90!
  • 5x Deceitful Chocolates for toppings
  • Legendary Magic Beascuit
  • The lead Jester himself, Shadow Milk Cookie takes third place tier in the meta! Shadow milk cookie is none other than a magic user cookie, he needs Searing Almonds to increase his cooldown stats, to use his special ability as often as possible within battle. At level 90, his cookie will be maxed out to his highest potential power. While his Beascuit will increase multiple stats at once, from anywhere from a 8% increase to a 15% increase in stats!
